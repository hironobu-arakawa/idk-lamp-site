I Don't Know Lamp

Concept

Is answering everything truly intelligent?

A truly wise partner knows its limits
and honestly seeks help when needed.

Choose an honest "I don't know" over a perfect answer.
It is in that small gap that trust between humans and AI is born.

Mechanism

The idk-lamp is not a warning light for errors.

It is a quiet signal of a baton passâ€”
where AI pauses to say, "From here, it is your turn,"
and relies on the human.

This may be the most sincere form of respect
a machine can show a human.

Philosophy

The Baton, Not Just the Calculation.

It is about designing the behavior
of yielding judgment to humans.

Let us build the circuit that lights the lamp.
That is our final responsibility as creators releasing AI into society.

Designed as a boundary artifact.
