Give AI

idk-lamp
(I Don’t Know Lamp)

When an AI system cannot decide, this signal lights up.

Skip intro

Scope

If we are to act with "Humans + AI"

AI is no longer just something on a screen.
We are already trying to act together with AI.

AI never "hesitates".
It can produce calculated answers, but it cannot tremble at uncertainty.
It cannot make bets, take responsibility, or even hold silence.

To accept anxiety and decide.
We will not surrender that human privilege to AI.

Philosophy

Place an "I Don't Know" lamp between Humans and AI

Is answering everything truly intelligent?
A truly smart partner is one who knows their limits
and honestly asks for help when needed.

A baton pass rather than a calculation result.
An honest "I don't know" rather than a perfect correct answer.

To give it the circuit to light a lamp, entrusting judgment to humans.
That is our responsibility.

Methodology

A record of searching together for where to stop

A "fray" is not a failure.
It is a "point" on the map showing that a boundary was needed there.

And connecting those points,
showing where to place the lamp, is the "map" called the repository.
Here we have prepared a record for practice and dialogue.

Action

First, a single signal

The "lamp" is just a metaphor.
It might be code returning return null,
or a single line of requirement saying "I want a boundary here".

The creator implements that function,
and the user raises their voice saying "Please attach that".

So, first, a signal for dialogue in that system.
