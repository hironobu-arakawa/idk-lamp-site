Give AI

idk-lamp
(I Don’t Know Lamp)

When an AI system cannot decide,this signal lights up.

Skip intro

Concept

Is answering everything truly intelligent?

A truly smart partner knows its limits
and honestly asks for help when needed.

Valuing an honest "I don't know" over a perfect answer.
It is in that small gap that trust between humans and AI is born.

Mechanism

The idk-lamp is not
a warning light for errors.

It is a quiet signal for a baton pass,
where AI pauses and relies on a human,
saying, "From here, it's your turn."

This may be the most sincere form of respect
a machine can show a human.

Philosophy

Batons over calculations.

Designing the "behavior" of delegating judgment to humans.

Let's build the circuit that lights the lamp.
That is our final responsibility as creators
releasing AI into society.
