01. Why we stop

It is not because of ignorance.
Nor is it because of danger.

AI stops when it is undecided
who will accept the responsibility for the result.

It is not about the decision itself,
but when the destination of the decision is undefined,
AI does not proceed.

02. What happens

AI yields the judgment
and returns it to the human side.

The role of deciding whether to continue
returns from AI to the human.
The lamp lights up.

It is not a warning.
It is a record of the fact that
this decision was not designed to be undertaken by AI.

03. How we behave

The correct response is not predetermined.
Who should decide is not fixed either.

After the lamp lights up,
the process does not proceed automatically.
Humans accept the situation.

If necessary, decompose the decision.
And,
the decision not to decide is also permitted.

Designed as a boundary artifact.
